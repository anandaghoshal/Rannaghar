About Us


Who are we?

Rannaghar is a home business dedicated to bringing Bengali flavors right to your doorstep. We send new menus every week and offer the option of either deliver, or pickup.


Why are we here?

We exist to spread culture, tradition, and love to the DFW metroplex so that they can enjoy our food with us! We offer a variety of food from just simple daal and rice, to dessert, to delicacies.
